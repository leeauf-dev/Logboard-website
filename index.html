FAQ

Longboard trick tracking without the mess.

What is Logboard?

Logboard is a longboarding app for tracking tricks, submitting clips, reviewing other riders and following longboard progression through points, trophies and rankings.

Is Logboard for beginners or advanced longboarders?

Both. Tricks are organized by category and level, so a rider can start with beginner tricks and keep moving toward advanced, expert and champion progression.

How does trick validation work?

You submit a longboard clip for a trick. Other riders review the video, and validated clips update your progression and rider profile.
